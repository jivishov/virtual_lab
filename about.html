About Virtual Laboratory

Project Overview

Virtual Laboratory is an open-source educational project that leverages modern AI capabilities to create interactive science simulations and virtual lab experiments. The project aims to make quality science education accessible to students worldwide, regardless of their access to physical laboratory facilities.

The Vision

Virtual experiments serve as pre-lab activities, similar to how flight simulators prepare pilots before actual flights. Each simulation guides users through every procedural step required in real laboratory settings, building confidence and understanding before hands-on work.

By providing virtual access to laboratory experiences, this project reaches communities where well-equipped lab facilities may not be available, ensuring all students can experience the complete experimental process.

Development Journey

The Virtual Laboratory project went live in 2025, following several years of development and refinement since the popularization of large language models in 2023. The journey involved numerous trials and iterations to harness AI coding capabilities effectively for educational purposes.

Technical Approach

All experiments and simulations are built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript exclusively. This deliberate choice ensures:

  • Easy deployment by educators worldwide
  • No complex dependencies or installation requirements
  • Compatibility with any modern web browser
  • Transparency through open-source code
  • Straightforward customization and adaptation

Classroom Integration

These virtual experiments are actively used in real classroom environments with Dr. Jivishov's students. Effective lesson plans and teaching guides will be added to the project in the near future, providing educators with comprehensive resources for classroom implementation.

Multilingual Accessibility

To maximize the project's positive impact, interfaces and procedures are being translated into various languages. This commitment to multilingual support ensures that language barriers don't prevent students from accessing quality science education.

About the Author

Dr. Emil Jivishov is the creator and lead developer of the Virtual Laboratory project. As an educator and researcher, Dr. Jivishov combines pedagogical expertise with modern technology to create effective learning tools that serve students across diverse educational contexts.

The project represents Dr. Jivishov's commitment to democratizing science education and ensuring that all students, regardless of location or resources, have access to meaningful laboratory experiences.
